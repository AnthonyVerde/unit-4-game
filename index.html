The Crystal Collector

You have been shown a random number below.
Each crystal has been assigned its own random value. Each time you click a crystal, its value will be added to your total score.
Your task is to click each crystal to add your total value up to match the random number displayed.
If you can add up to the number shown, you win! If your total goes over, you lose!
Lose five times, the game will reset and you'll have to try all over again. Win five times, you'll be a Crystal Collector master and the game will reset.
Enjoy!
